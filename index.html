Geovisualization Portfolio Isaac Young GEOG:3540

In this web page, you will see a variety of geovisualization projects I have completed for GEOG:3540 at the University of Iowa.

The projects were developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a number of visualization and mapping libraries tools such as CartoDB, Leaflet, and D3.

Iowa Geographical and Sustainability Sciences

Home Internet Access in California

This map shows home internet access by California counties. Households without home internet access are those without in-home wifi.

Large Swine Feeding Operations in Iowa

The number of large Swine Animal Feeding Operations (AFOs) in 2017 by Iowa county. Large AFOs are those with 500 or more "heads" or indivudal swine.

California Internet Access and Percent White

The percent of households without access to home internet and the percent white population by California county. This map shows the bivariate relationship between these two variables.

Internal Migration in China

A flow map showing migration between Chinese provinces from 2005 to 2015 made using flowmapper.org. Migrations shown are only those that are internal, so from province to province.

Annual Precipitation Map Iowa

Isarithmic map of annual precipitation for the state of Iowa.

Bur Oak Blight Multivariate Analysis

A final group project for GEOG:3540 at the University of Iowa. Using SOMVIS, we visualized connections between multiple variables and how they may contribute to BOB.
